On this webpage I will post important links for the tutorial and homework, so that there is one place, where you can find everything at once. I will try to keep this website as up to date as possible.

If any questions concerning the homework or the tutorial come up, then I do suggest sending me a mail (peter.mortimer@tum.de) or posting your questions onto the moodle forum. We also suggest using Filezilla to easily move your C files from your desktop to your remote ssh connection. And from there then you can compile your code and push your files via git to your repository.
